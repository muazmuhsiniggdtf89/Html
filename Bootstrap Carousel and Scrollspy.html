Overview

The Blue Economy refers to the sustainable use of ocean resources for economic growth, improved livelihoods, and jobs while preserving the health of ocean ecosystem.

Importance

The Blue Economy is crucial for food security, job creation, and sustainable development.

Challenges

Challenges include overfishing, pollution, and climate change affecting marine ecosystems.

Opportunities

Opportunities in renewable energy, sustainable fisheries, and marine tourism can drive the Blue Economy forward.

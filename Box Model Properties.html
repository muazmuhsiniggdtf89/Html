Real Madrid Club de Fútbol

Real Madrid, based in Madrid, Spain, is one of the most successful and storied football clubs in the history of the sport. Founded on March 6, 1902, the club has earned a reputation for excellence, ambition, and a legacy built on winning.

Club Achievements

Real Madrid has an incredible trophy cabinet, including:

La Liga titles: Over 35 times (most in Spain)

UEFA Champions League titles: 14 (most in Europe)

Copa del Rey wins: 20+

FIFA Club World Cups: 5

Their dominance in European competition, especially the Champions League, sets them apart from other clubs.

Stadium: Santiago Bernabéu

Home to Real Madrid since 1947, the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium is a symbol of prestige and footballing heritage. Located in the heart of Madrid, it's undergoing a major renovation to become one of the most advanced stadiums in the world.

Legendary Players

Real Madrid has been home to some of the greatest footballers in history, including:

  • Alfredo Di Stéfano
  • Ferenc Puskás
  • Zinedine Zidane
  • Cristiano Ronaldo (club’s all-time top scorer)
  • Raúl
  • Iker Casillas
  • Sergio Ramos
  • Luka Modrić
  • Karim Benzema

These stars helped Real Madrid dominate both domestically and internationally.

The Galácticos Era

Under President Florentino Pérez, Real Madrid became famous for signing global superstars in what became known as the "Galácticos" era. This strategy brought players like Figo, Ronaldo Nazário, Beckham, and Zidane to the club, combining talent with massive global appeal.

Current Squad (2024–2025)

The club continues to evolve with a mix of young talent and experienced stars. Players like Jude Bellingham, Vinícius Júnior, Rodrygo, Aurélien Tchouaméni, and Eduardo Camavinga represent the next generation of stars, supported by veterans like Toni Kroos and Luka Modrić.

Club Philosophy and Identity

Real Madrid stands for ambition, professionalism, and a relentless pursuit of victory. The club's motto, "Hala Madrid y Nada Más" (Hala Madrid and Nothing More), reflects its passionate fanbase and singular focus on excellence.
